# VPN Connection Issue Workflow

## Issue
User cannot connect to VPN from home.

## Troubleshooting Steps
1. Confirm internet connectivity.
2. Verify VPN client is installed and updated.
3. Check user’s VPN permissions in AD.
4. Restart VPN service.
5. Attempt reconnection.

## Root Cause
Outdated VPN client or incorrect credentials.

## Resolution
Updated client and re-authenticated user.
